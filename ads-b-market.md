<p><p>The ADS-B market is divided into two primary types: ADS-B Out and ADS-B In. ADS-B Out refers to the systems on aircraft that broadcast location, speed, and other data to ground stations and other aircraft, enhancing situational awareness and air traffic management. In contrast, ADS-B In involves receiving these broadcasts, allowing aircraft operators to access real-time information about their surroundings, improving safety and operational efficiency. Both types are critical for modern aviation surveillance and navigation systems.</p></p>
