<p><p>The animal-based compound feed market includes various forms like mash, pellets, crumble, and others. Mash is a finely ground mixture of raw materials, ideal for young animals due to its digestibility. Pellets are compressed and shaped feed, offering convenience, reduced waste, and balanced nutrition. Crumble is a coarser, granulated form, often used for poultry as a transition between mash and pellets. Other forms include extruded or liquid feeds, tailored for specific animal needs, ensuring optimal growth, health, and production efficiency.</p></p>
