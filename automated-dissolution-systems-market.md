<p><p>Automated dissolution systems are essential in pharmaceutical testing, categorized into online and offline systems. Online dissolution systems continuously monitor and analyze samples during the dissolution process, providing real-time data for immediate assessment and adjustments. In contrast, offline dissolution systems involve extracting samples at intervals for analysis externally, offering a more traditional approach. Both systems enhance testing accuracy and efficiency, but online systems tend to offer faster results and improved process control, while offline systems may be perceived as simpler and more familiar.</p></p>
