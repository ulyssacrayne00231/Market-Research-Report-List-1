<p><ul><li>Passive Car Alarm</li><li>Active Car Alarm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive alarms market is primarily classified into passive and active car alarms. Passive car alarms automatically activate when the vehicle is turned off, providing continuous protection without user intervention. They often include features like immobilizers and shock sensors. In contrast, active car alarms require manual arming and disarming, allowing vehicle owners to control when the system is active. These alarms typically include remote key fobs and visual alerts, providing enhanced security through user engagement. Both types aim to deter theft and enhance vehicle safety.</p></p>
