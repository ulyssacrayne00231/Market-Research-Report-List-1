<p><p>The Automotive Evaporative Emission Control (EVAP) System prevents fuel vapors from escaping into the atmosphere. Key components include the **Fuel Tank**, which stores fuel and traps vapors; the **Gas Cap**, sealing the tank to prevent vapor leaks; the **Liquid Vapor Separator**, which separates liquid fuel from vapors; the **Canister**, storing vapors until they are purged; the **Control Valve**, regulating vapor flow; **Sensors**, monitoring system pressure and leaks; and **Hoses**, connecting components to ensure proper vapor flow and system functionality. Together, they reduce emissions and enhance environmental compliance.</p></p>
