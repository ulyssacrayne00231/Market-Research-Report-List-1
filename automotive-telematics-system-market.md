<p><p>Automotive telematics systems encompass three primary market types: Embedded, Tethered, and Integrated. Embedded systems are built directly into the vehicle's architecture, providing seamless functionality and connectivity without relying on external devices. Tethered systems connect through smartphones or other portable devices, offering flexibility but requiring a constant external link. Integrated systems blend embedded and tethered features, enhancing user experience by combining onboard hardware with external connectivity, allowing for advanced services like navigation, vehicle tracking, and diagnostics.</p></p>
