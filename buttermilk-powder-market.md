<p><strong>The Buttermilk Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spray Dried Buttermilk Powder</li><li>Atmospheric Roller Dried Buttermilk Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The buttermilk powder market consists primarily of two types: spray-dried and atmospheric roller-dried buttermilk powder. Spray-dried buttermilk powder involves a process where liquid buttermilk is sprayed into a hot drying chamber, resulting in fine powder with retained flavor and nutritional value. In contrast, atmospheric roller-dried buttermilk powder is produced by spreading buttermilk onto heated rollers, allowing it to dry and form sheets that are then ground into powder. Both types serve various applications in food processing, baking, and culinary uses.</p></p>
