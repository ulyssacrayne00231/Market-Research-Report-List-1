<p><p>The carbon fiber filament market primarily includes two types of processing methods: injection molding and extrusion molding. Injection molding involves melting the carbon fiber composite and injecting it into a predefined mold, allowing for complex shapes and high-volume production. Extrusion molding, on the other hand, involves forcing the molten material through a die to create continuous profiles or sheets, suitable for various applications. Both methods leverage the superior strength and lightweight properties of carbon fiber, catering to diverse industries.</p></p>
