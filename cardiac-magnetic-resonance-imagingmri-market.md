<p><p>Cardiac Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) is a non-invasive imaging technique used to assess heart anatomy and function. The market is segmented into two primary types: closed system and open system. Closed system MRI offers higher magnetic field strength, providing superior image resolution but requires the patient to be enclosed in a tube-like structure. In contrast, open system MRI features an open design, enhancing patient comfort and accessibility, though it may deliver lower image quality. Both systems cater to diverse patient needs and clinical requirements.</p></p>
