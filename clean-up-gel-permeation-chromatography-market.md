<p><p>The Clean-up Gel Permeation Chromatography (GPC) market is categorized into high temperature and room temperature segments. High-temperature GPC involves elevated thermal conditions to enhance polymer solubility and reduce viscosity, allowing for better separation of macromolecules. This method is ideal for complex samples and provides faster analysis. In contrast, room-temperature GPC operates under ambient conditions, making it suitable for sensitive materials that may degrade at higher temperatures. Both types cater to specific applications in polymer analysis and material characterization.</p></p>
