<p><p>The composites piezoelectric material market is primarily categorized into two types: 1-3 and 2-2. The 1-3 type features a configuration where piezoelectric fibers are embedded in a non-piezoelectric matrix, enhancing flexibility and application in sensors and actuators. In contrast, the 2-2 type comprises piezoelectric elements arranged in a parallel configuration, offering higher mechanical rigidity and is often utilized in transducer applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, aerospace, and medical devices, driving market growth.</p></p>
