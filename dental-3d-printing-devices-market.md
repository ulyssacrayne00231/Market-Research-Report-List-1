<p><strong>The Dental 3D Printing Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Digital Light Projection</li><li>3D Jet Printing</li><li>Stereo Lithography Apparatus (SLA) 3D Printing</li><li>Selective Laser Sintering (SLS)</li><li>Direct Laser Metal Sintering (DLMS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental 3D printing devices market encompasses various technologies, each suited for different applications. Digital Light Projection (DLP) uses light to cure resin, allowing for fast production of dental models. 3D Jet Printing deposits material layer by layer, ideal for creating complex structures. Stereolithography Apparatus (SLA) offers high-resolution prints with photopolymerization. Selective Laser Sintering (SLS) uses a laser to fuse powdered materials, ideal for durable parts, while Direct Laser Metal Sintering (DLMS) focuses on 3D metal printing, catering to custom dental restorations.</p></p>
