<p><strong>The Dry-type Capacitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Filling</li><li>Semi-solid Filling</li><li>Gas Filling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dry-type capacitor market is segmented into three types based on their filling materials. Solid filling capacitors utilize solid dielectric materials for insulation and energy storage, offering high reliability and durability. Semi-solid filling capacitors combine solid and liquid mediums, enhancing performance characteristics and thermal management. Gas filling capacitors, on the other hand, use inert gas to prevent dielectric breakdown and improve efficiency, making them suitable for high-stress applications. Each type serves distinct applications based on performance, safety, and environmental considerations.</p></p>
