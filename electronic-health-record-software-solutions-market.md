<p><p>The Electronic Health Record (EHR) software solutions market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based EHRs are hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and reduced IT maintenance costs, making them accessible from anywhere with internet connectivity. On-premise EHRs, on the other hand, are installed locally within a healthcare facility, providing more control over data security and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. Both types cater to diverse healthcare needs and preferences.</p></p>
