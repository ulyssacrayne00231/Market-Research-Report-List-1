<p><strong>The Emergency Shut-off Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Motorised Valve</li><li>Solenoid Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Emergency Shut-off Valve market comprises primarily two types: motorised valves and solenoid valves. Motorised valves are actuated by an electric motor that enables precise control and rapid response to emergency situations, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. In contrast, solenoid valves use an electromagnetic coil to control fluid flow, allowing for quick shut-off in emergencies with minimal power consumption. Both types are essential for safety and automation across industries such as oil and gas, chemical processing, and manufacturing.</p></p>
