<p><strong>The Endogenous Creatinine Assay Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Jaffe's Kinetic Method</li><li>Enzymatic Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Endogenous Creatinine Assay Kits market is primarily categorized into two types: the Jaffe's Kinetic Method and the Enzymatic Method. The Jaffe's Kinetic Method measures creatinine concentration through a colorimetric reaction, providing rapid results but may be influenced by interfering substances. In contrast, the Enzymatic Method uses specific enzymes to convert creatinine to a measurable product, offering higher specificity and reduced interference. These methods cater to varying clinical and research needs, optimizing creatinine measurement in blood and urine samples.</p></p>
