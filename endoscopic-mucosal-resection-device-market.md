<p><p>The Endoscopic Mucosal Resection Device market includes three primary types: Entry Needles, Mucosectomy snares, and Probes. Entry Needles facilitate the initial access to the targeted mucosal area for resection, allowing for precise injection and lifting of tissue. Mucosectomy snares are specialized tools designed to excise larger mucosal lesions effectively. Probes are utilized for advanced diagnostic or therapeutic procedures, enhancing precision in mucosal investigations and resections. Together, these devices support minimally invasive techniques in gastrointestinal treatments.</p></p>
