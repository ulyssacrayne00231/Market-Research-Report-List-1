<p><p>The enteral stents market comprises various types designed for specific gastrointestinal applications. Gastroduodenal stents facilitate food passage between the stomach and duodenum, often employed in cases of gastric outlet obstruction. Colon stents are utilized to treat colorectal obstructions, providing a minimally invasive option for restoring bowel function. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing patient quality of life by addressing critical digestive issues.</p></p>
