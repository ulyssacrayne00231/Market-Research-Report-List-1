<p><p>The Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) cooler market includes Tube EGR Coolers and Finned EGR Coolers. Tube EGR Coolers use a series of tubes to transfer heat from exhaust gases to coolant, offering durability and efficient heat exchange. Finned EGR Coolers incorporate fins to increase surface area, enhancing heat dissipation and improving cooling efficiency. Both types reduce nitrogen oxide emissions by cooling exhaust gases before recirculation, but finned coolers are often preferred for higher performance in demanding applications, while tube coolers are valued for their robustness.</p></p>
