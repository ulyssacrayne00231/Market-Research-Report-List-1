<p><p>Flexible packaging for food and beverages encompasses various materials, primarily including PET (polyethylene terephthalate), PE (polyethylene), and PP (polypropylene). PET is known for its strength and barrier properties, making it ideal for bottling beverages. PE is widely used for pouches and wraps due to its moisture resistance and flexibility. PP offers excellent heat resistance and is often utilized for packaging snacks and ready-to-eat meals. Other materials include paper and compostable films, catering to eco-friendly consumer preferences.</p></p>
