<p><p>The food waste disposer market consists mainly of two types: pulverizing and drying. Pulverizing type disposers efficiently grind food waste into small particles, which can be easily disposed of through drains or composted. In contrast, drying type disposers transform food waste by removing moisture, resulting in dehydrated material that can be safely discarded or converted to animal feed. Both types cater to the growing demand for sustainable waste management solutions, minimizing landfill impact and promoting recycling efforts.</p></p>
