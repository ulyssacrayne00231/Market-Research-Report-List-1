<p><strong>The G-line Photoresist Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photopolymerization</li><li>Light Decomposition Type</li><li>Photocrosslinked</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The G-line photoresist market comprises three primary types: photopolymerization, light decomposition type, and photocrosslinked. Photopolymerization refers to the process where light exposure initiates a chemical reaction, forming a solid polymer structure. Light decomposition type involves a chemical breakdown of components when exposed to specific light wavelengths, thereby altering solubility. Photocrosslinked photoresists create a network of interconnected polymer chains, enhancing durability and thermal stability. Each type serves distinct applications in semiconductor fabrication and microelectronics, influencing the overall market dynamics.</p></p>
