<p><p>Gamma detectors are categorized into several types based on their detection mechanisms. Scintillation detectors use materials that emit light when exposed to gamma radiation, providing fast response times and high sensitivity, ideal for various applications. Semiconductor-based detectors, such as cadmium telluride or silicon, offer excellent energy resolution and compact size, making them suitable for precise measurements.
