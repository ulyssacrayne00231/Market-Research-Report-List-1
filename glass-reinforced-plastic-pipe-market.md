<p><p>Glass Reinforced Plastic (GRP) pipes, also known as fiberglass-reinforced plastic pipes, are composite materials made of a polymer matrix reinforced with glass fibers. These pipes are known for their high strength-to-weight ratio, corrosion resistance, and durability, making them ideal for applications in industries such as water and wastewater management, oil and gas, chemical processing, and irrigation. GRP pipes are lightweight, easy to install, and have a long service life, which reduces maintenance costs.</p><p>The Glass Reinforced Plastic Pipe Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.7% during the forecast period.
