<p><p>Heterojunction modules are advanced solar technology that combines different semiconductor materials to enhance efficiency. The market is primarily divided into two types: N-type and P-type. N-type modules utilize silicon with extra electrons, providing higher efficiency and better performance in low-light conditions. In contrast, P-type modules use silicon with a deficiency of electrons, generally being more cost-effective and widely adopted. Both types offer unique benefits, catering to diverse customer preferences and application requirements in the solar energy sector.</p></p>
