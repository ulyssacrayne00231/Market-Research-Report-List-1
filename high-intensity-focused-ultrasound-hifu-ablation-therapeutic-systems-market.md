<p><p>High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) Ablation Therapeutic Systems encompass two primary market types: ultrasound-guided and MRI-guided. Ultrasound-guided HIFU utilizes real-time ultrasound imaging to precisely target and ablate tissues, making it suitable for various applications such as tumor treatment. MRI-guided HIFU, on the other hand, employs magnetic resonance imaging for improved visualization and planning, enhancing accuracy during procedures. Both technologies leverage focused ultrasound energy to selectively destroy abnormal tissues, offering non-invasive alternatives in medical therapies.</p></p>
