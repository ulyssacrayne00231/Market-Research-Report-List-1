<p><strong>The Horizontal End Suction Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Frame Mounted End Suction Pump</li><li>Close Coupled End Suction Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The horizontal end suction pump market includes two main types: frame mounted end suction pumps and close coupled end suction pumps. Frame mounted end suction pumps are installed on a separate base and allow for easier maintenance and modification, making them suitable for larger applications. Close coupled end suction pumps, on the other hand, have the motor directly mounted to the pump housing, resulting in a compact design ideal for space-constrained environments. Both types are widely used in various industrial applications.</p></p>
