<p><strong>The Hot Runner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Gate Hot Runner</li><li>Valve Gate Hot Runner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hot runner market is divided primarily into two types: Open Gate and Valve Gate systems. Open Gate hot runners allow molten plastic to flow freely into the mold cavity, enabling quicker cycle times and simpler design. In contrast, Valve Gate hot runners utilize a valve mechanism to control the flow of plastic, providing precise shot control and reduced waste. Both types are integral in enhancing efficiency and quality in injection molding processes, catering to different production requirements and applications.</p></p>
