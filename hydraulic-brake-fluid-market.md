<p><p>The hydraulic brake fluid market is categorized into various types based on performance characteristics and compatibility. DOT 3 fluid, with a lower boiling point, is commonly used in older vehicles. DOT 4 offers improved performance and higher boiling points, suitable for modern cars. DOT 5 is silicone-based and not compatible with other types, primarily used in specialty applications. DOT 5.1, while similar to DOT 4, has a higher boiling point and is compatible with glycol-based fluids, making it versatile for high-performance vehicles.</p></p>
