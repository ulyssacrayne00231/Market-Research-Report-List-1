<p><p>The hydraulic watertight doors market is divided into two main types: heavy watertight doors and light watertight doors. Heavy watertight doors are designed for industrial and marine applications, providing robust protection against water ingress in high-pressure environments. They are typically used in ships, submarines, and offshore facilities. In contrast, light watertight doors are suitable for less demanding applications, offering adequate water resistance while being easier to operate and install. These doors are commonly found in commercial buildings and smaller vessels.</p></p>
