<p><p>The ice melt salt market comprises two primary types: liquid and solid ice melt salt. Liquid ice melt salt is typically comprised of brine solutions that are easily spread and activate quickly upon application, making them ideal for pre-treatment of surfaces. In contrast, solid ice melt salt includes granular materials like sodium chloride or calcium chloride, which provide a slower, sustained melting action and are effective for larger surfaces. Both types are essential for maintaining safe, ice-free environments during winter conditions.</p></p>
