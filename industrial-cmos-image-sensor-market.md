<p><strong>The Industrial CMOS Image Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FSI</li><li>BSI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial CMOS Image Sensor market is primarily categorized into two types: Front-Side Illuminated (FSI) and Back-Side Illuminated (BSI) sensors. FSI sensors capture light from the front, making them ideal for applications with sufficient lighting, while BSI sensors have a rear-illuminated design that improves performance in low-light conditions by allowing more light to reach the photodiodes. These technologies cater to diverse industrial needs, from machine vision to robotics, enhancing image quality and sensitivity across various applications.</p></p>
