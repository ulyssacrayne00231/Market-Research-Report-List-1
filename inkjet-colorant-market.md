<p><strong>The Inkjet Colorant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dye Inkjet Colorant</li><li>Pigment Inkjet Colorant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inkjet colorant market comprises two main types: dye inkjet colorants and pigment inkjet colorants. Dye inkjet colorants offer vibrant colors and are easily absorbed by paper, making them ideal for high-quality photo printing. However, they are less resistant to UV light and water. In contrast, pigment inkjet colorants provide better durability and resistance to fading and water, making them suitable for archival purposes and outdoor applications. Each type serves distinct printing needs based on desired longevity and color quality.</p></p>
