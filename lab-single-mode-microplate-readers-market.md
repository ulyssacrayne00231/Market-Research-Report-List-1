<p><ul><li>Fluorescence Plate Readers</li><li>Absorbance Plate Readers</li><li>Luminescence Plate Readers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lab single-mode microplate readers are essential instruments used in various research and clinical applications for analyzing biological samples. Fluorescence plate readers measure the emitted light from fluorescent compounds, making them ideal for sensitive detection in assays. Absorbance plate readers quantify how much light is absorbed by samples at specific wavelengths, commonly used in enzyme-linked immunosorbent assays (ELISA). Luminescence plate readers detect light produced from chemical reactions, often employed in bioluminescent assays and cellular studies, offering high sensitivity and specificity.</p></p>
