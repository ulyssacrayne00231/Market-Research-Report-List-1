<p><p>The lab vacuum pump market comprises various types, primarily including gas delivery pumps and gas adsorption pumps. Gas delivery pumps are designed to create a vacuum by efficiently removing gases from a chamber, facilitating processes like evaporation and distillation. In contrast, gas adsorption pumps utilize materials that physically adsorb gas molecules, effectively reducing pressure within a system. Both types serve critical roles in laboratory applications, enhancing precision and efficiency in experiments and processes requiring controlled environments.</p></p>
