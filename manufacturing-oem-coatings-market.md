<p><p>The Manufacturing (OEM) Coatings market comprises various types, including water-based, solvent-based, powder coatings, and radiation-curable coatings. Water-based coatings are eco-friendly and low in volatile organic compounds, making them safer for both users and the environment. Solvent-based coatings offer excellent durability and finish. Powder coatings are dry and applied electrostatically, providing a hard, resilient finish suitable for various surfaces. Radiation-curable coatings leverage UV or electron beam technology to achieve rapid curing, offering efficient application and high-performance results.</p></p>
