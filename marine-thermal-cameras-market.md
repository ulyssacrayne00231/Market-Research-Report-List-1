<p><p>The marine thermal cameras market comprises two main types: uncooled and cooled thermal cameras. Uncooled thermal cameras operate at ambient temperatures, utilizing materials that generate thermal images without the need for complex cooling systems, making them cost-effective and compact. In contrast, cooled thermal cameras use cryogenic cooling technology to enhance thermal sensitivity, allowing for superior image clarity and detection capabilities, particularly in challenging environments. Both types cater to diverse marine applications, including surveillance, navigation, and search-and-rescue operations.</p></p>
