<p><p>The metal complex dyes market is categorized into two main types: 1:1 metal-complexes and 1:2 metal-complexes. 1:1 metal-complexes involve a single metal ion binding with a dye molecule, providing moderate hue stability and wash fastness. In contrast, 1:2 metal-complexes feature two dye molecules coordinated to one metal ion, resulting in enhanced color intensity and improved fastness properties. These distinctions influence applications in textiles, leather, and other industries, where performance and aesthetic qualities are critical.</p></p>
