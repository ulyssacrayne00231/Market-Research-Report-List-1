<p><p>Microcrystalline wax is categorized based on its hardness and melting point, with common grades being 75#, 85#, and 90#. The 75# grade features softer characteristics, making it suitable for applications like cosmetics and food packaging. The 85# grade offers a medium hardness, ideal for candle production and industrial uses. The 90# grade is the hardest, providing enhanced stability for specialized applications in coatings, adhesives, and high-performance formulations. Each grade serves distinct industries and consumer needs.</p></p>
