In contrast, oil-soluble microgold consists of gold nanoparticles that mix well with oils, ideal for cosmetics and nutritional supplements. Both types leverage the beneficial properties of gold, including antimicrobial effects and bioavailability, catering to diverse consumer needs in health and beauty industries.</p></p>
