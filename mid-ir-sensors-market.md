<p><strong>The Mid IR Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heat Detector</li><li>Photon Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mid IR sensors market encompasses two primary types: heat detectors and photon detectors. Heat detectors identify temperature changes, providing alerts in various applications like fire detection and industrial monitoring. They operate by sensing thermal radiation emitted by objects. Photon detectors, on the other hand, detect infrared radiation at mid-wavelengths, crucial for spectroscopy, environmental monitoring, and advanced imaging systems. Both types play vital roles in security, climate research, and process control, reflecting the growing demand for precise and efficient sensing technologies.</p></p>
