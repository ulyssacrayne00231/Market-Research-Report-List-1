<p><p>The mineral wool composite market primarily encompasses two types: rock wool composite and glass wool composite. Rock wool composite, made from volcanic rock and lime, offers excellent thermal insulation and fire resistance, making it ideal for industrial applications. Glass wool composite, produced from recycled glass fibers, is lightweight and provides effective thermal insulation, often used in residential and commercial buildings. Both types are valued for their sustainability, energy efficiency, and soundproofing capabilities, contributing to the growing demand in the construction industry.</p></p>
