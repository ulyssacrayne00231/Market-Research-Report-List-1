<p><strong>The Nickel-based Alloys Clad Pipes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roll Bonding Process</li><li>Mechanically</li><li>Explosive Bonding Process</li><li>Weld Overlay Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nickel-based alloys clad pipes are produced using various bonding processes. The roll bonding process involves applying pressure to fuse layers, ensuring strong adhesion. Mechanically clad pipes use mechanical interlocking for a durable bond. The explosive bonding process utilizes controlled explosions to create a metallurgical bond, ideal for dissimilar materials. Lastly, the weld overlay process involves welding a layer of nickel-based alloy onto a base pipe, enhancing corrosion resistance and strength. Each method offers unique advantages for diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
