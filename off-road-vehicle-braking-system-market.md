<p><strong>The Off-road Vehicle Braking System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The off-road vehicle braking system market includes two main types: Two-Wheel Braking System and Four-Wheel Braking System. Two-wheel systems primarily apply braking force to either the front or rear wheels, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for lighter off-road vehicles. Four-wheel systems distribute braking force across all wheels, providing superior control, stability, and stopping power, especially in challenging terrains. Both systems are designed to enhance safety and performance, with the choice depending on vehicle type, terrain, and user requirements.</p></p>
