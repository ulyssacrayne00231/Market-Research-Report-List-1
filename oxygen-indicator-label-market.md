<p><p>The oxygen indicator label market is segmented into two main types: reversible and non-reversible oxygen indicator labels. Reversible oxygen indicator labels can change color in response to fluctuating oxygen levels, allowing for real-time monitoring and adjustment of conditions. In contrast, non-reversible oxygen indicator labels show a permanent color change once exposed to oxygen, providing a one-time readout that indicates a threshold has been exceeded. Both types serve critical roles in packaging and preserving sensitive products by ensuring optimal storage conditions.</p></p>
