<p><p>The Passenger Count System market includes Infrared, Time-of-Flight, and Stereoscopic Vision types. Infrared systems use heat sensors to detect passengers, offering reliability in low-light conditions. Time-of-Flight systems measure distance using light pulses, providing high accuracy and real-time data. Stereoscopic Vision systems use dual cameras to create 3D depth maps, enabling precise counting even in crowded spaces. Each type caters to diverse needs, balancing accuracy, cost, and environmental adaptability, making them essential for public transport, retail, and smart city applications.</p></p>
