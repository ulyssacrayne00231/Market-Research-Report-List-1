<p><p>A Pneumatic Anti-Lock Braking System (ABS) is a safety mechanism designed for vehicles, particularly heavy-duty ones like trucks and buses, that use pneumatic (air) braking systems. It prevents wheel lock-up during sudden braking by modulating brake pressure, ensuring better vehicle control and reducing stopping distances. This system enhances safety, especially on slippery or uneven surfaces, and is widely adopted in commercial vehicles to comply with stringent safety regulations.</p><p>The Pneumatic Anti-Lock Braking System (ABS) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.2% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by increasing demand for advanced safety features in commercial vehicles, stricter government regulations mandating ABS installation, and rising awareness about road safety. The expansion of the logistics and transportation industry, particularly in emerging economies, further fuels market growth.</p><p>Recent trends in the market include the integration of ABS with advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) and the development of lightweight, cost-effective ABS solutions. Additionally, the shift toward electric and autonomous commercial vehicles is creating new opportunities for ABS manufacturers to innovate and adapt their products. The market is also witnessing increased collaboration between automotive manufacturers and ABS suppliers to enhance system efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
