<p><ul><li>Condensation Polymers</li><li>Addition Polymers</li><li>Hybrid Polymers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyimide binders market includes three main types: condensation polymers, addition polymers, and hybrid polymers. Condensation polymers are formed through the elimination of small molecules during polymerization and offer excellent thermal stability. Addition polymers, created by the direct addition of monomers without byproducts, provide versatility and ease of processing. Hybrid polymers combine features from both types, enhancing performance and adaptability. Together, these categories cater to various applications in aerospace, electronics, and automotive industries, driving demand for high-performance materials.</p></p>
