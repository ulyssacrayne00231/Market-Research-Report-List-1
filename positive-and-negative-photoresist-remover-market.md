<p><p>Positive and negative photoresist removers are chemical solutions used in the semiconductor and electronics industries to clean substrates by removing photoresist materials applied during the photolithography process. Positive photoresist removers are designed to dissolve the positive photoresist, leaving the developed pattern intact. In contrast, negative photoresist removers are formulated to effectively dissolve negative photoresists, which solidify upon exposure to light, allowing for precise etching processes.</p><p>The Positive and Negative Photoresist Remover Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.7% during the forecast period.
