<p><strong>The Positive Displacement Sanitary Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary Positive Displacement Sanitary Pumps</li><li>Reciprocating Positive Displacement Sanitary Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Positive Displacement Sanitary Pumps market is categorized into two main types: rotary and reciprocating. Rotary positive displacement sanitary pumps use rotating mechanisms to move fluid, ensuring a continuous flow suitable for viscous materials in hygiene-sensitive applications. In contrast, reciprocating positive displacement sanitary pumps rely on a back-and-forth motion of a piston or diaphragm to deliver precise volumes, making them ideal for applications requiring high pressure and accuracy. Both types are essential for industries demanding strict sanitary standards, such as food, beverage, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
