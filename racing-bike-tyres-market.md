<p><strong>The Racing Bike Tyres Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Clincher Bike Tyres</li><li>Tubular Bike Tyres</li><li>Tubeless Bike Tyres</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The racing bike tyre market features three main types: clincher, tubular, and tubeless tyres. Clincher tyres, equipped with a separate inner tube, are popular for their ease of repair and versatility. Tubular tyres, sewn around an inner tube, provide a secure fit and lower rolling resistance, favored by professional racers. Tubeless tyres eliminate the inner tube, reducing puncture risks and allowing for lower pressure, enhancing grip and comfort. Each type offers distinct advantages, catering to diverse racing preferences and performance needs.</p></p>
