<p><p>The Reverse Osmosis (RO) and Nanofiltration (NF) membranes market can be categorized based on the technology employed. RO technology primarily focuses on desalination and removing impurities from water, utilizing semi-permeable membranes that allow solvent molecules to pass while blocking contaminants. On the other hand, NF technology targets larger molecular compounds and divalent ions with slightly larger pores, making it suitable for applications like water softening and organic compound removal. Both technologies play a crucial role in water treatment and purity enhancement.</p></p>
