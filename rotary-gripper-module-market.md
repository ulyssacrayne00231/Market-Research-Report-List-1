<p><ul><li>Parallel</li><li>Angular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rotary Gripper Module market is primarily divided into two types: Parallel and Angular grippers. Parallel grippers maintain parallel jaw motion, allowing for consistent gripping of objects with uniform surfaces, making them ideal for handling flat or rectangular items. Angular grippers, on the other hand, utilize a pivoting motion, enabling them to grasp objects at varying angles, which is advantageous for irregularly shaped items or when access space is limited. Both types cater to diverse automation needs in industrial applications.</p></p>
