<p><p>The smoke alarm controllers market is divided into two primary types: wireless controllers and wired controllers. Wireless controllers utilize radio frequency or Wi-Fi technology, enabling easy installation and flexibility in positioning throughout a space. They are ideal for retrofitting existing buildings. In contrast, wired controllers are connected directly to a building's electrical system, offering a reliable, continuous power source and often higher durability. Each type serves specific needs, influencing installation choices based on cost, complexity, and location requirements.</p></p>
