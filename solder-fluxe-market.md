<p><ul><li>Water Soluble</li><li>No-Clean</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solder flux market is categorized into three main types: water-soluble, no-clean, and other fluxes. Water-soluble fluxes require cleaning after soldering due to their residues, making them suitable for applications where thorough cleaning is necessary. No-clean fluxes leave minimal residues that do not require removal, ideal for soldering processes where cleanliness is crucial but cleaning is impractical. Other flux types include specific formulations for unique applications, catering to various industry needs such as automotive, electronics, and telecom.</p></p>
