<p><p>The Spine Cage Market is categorized into four main types: Lumbar, Cervical, Thoracolumbar, and Thoracic. Lumbar spine cages are designed for the lower back to support spinal fusion. Cervical cages are used for the neck region, stabilizing vertebrae after injury or surgery. Thoracolumbar cages address issues in both the thoracic and lumbar regions, while Thoracic cages are specifically for the upper and mid-back. Each type serves distinct anatomical regions and patient needs, enhancing surgical outcomes and recovery.</p></p>
