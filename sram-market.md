<p><strong>The SRAM Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>nvSRAM</li><li>Asynchronous SRAM</li><li>Synchronous SRAM</li><li>Low Power SRAM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SRAM market encompasses various types tailored for specific applications. nvSRAM combines non-volatility with high-speed access, ideal for data retention in power failure scenarios. Asynchronous SRAM operates without a clock signal, making it suitable for simpler, low-latency applications. Synchronous SRAM, synchronized with the system clock, supports higher data rates, making it ideal for high-performance computing tasks. Low Power SRAM focuses on minimal energy consumption, targeting mobile and battery-operated devices, enhancing efficiency while maintaining reliable performance.</p></p>
