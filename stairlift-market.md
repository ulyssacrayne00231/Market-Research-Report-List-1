<p><strong>The Stairlift Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight Stairlift</li><li>Curved Stairlift</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stairlift market is primarily divided into two types: straight stairlifts and curved stairlifts. Straight stairlifts are designed for straight staircases, featuring a simple track system for easy installation and lower costs. In contrast, curved stairlifts are tailored for staircases with bends or landings, requiring custom tracks and more complex installation, resulting in higher prices. Both types enhance mobility and accessibility, catering to individuals with limited mobility, but they serve different architectural needs and user requirements.</p></p>
