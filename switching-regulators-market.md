<p><p>The switching regulators market includes buck and boost regulators, essential for efficient voltage conversion in electronic devices. Buck regulators step down input voltage, making them ideal for powering low-voltage components while maximizing energy efficiency. In contrast, boost regulators step up voltage, supplying higher voltage to devices from a lower input. Both types are widely used in consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial applications, driven by the need for energy efficiency and compact designs in an increasingly power-sensitive market.</p></p>
