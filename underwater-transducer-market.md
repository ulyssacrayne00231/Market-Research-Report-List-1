<p><strong>The Underwater Transducer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Frequency</li><li>Intermediate Frequency</li><li>High Frequency</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The underwater transducer market is categorized by frequency ranges: low frequency (below 1 kHz), intermediate frequency (1 kHz to 50 kHz), and high frequency (above 50 kHz). Low-frequency transducers are ideal for long-range sonar applications, penetrating deeper waters and detecting larger objects. Intermediate-frequency transducers balance range and resolution, suitable for fishing and marine research. High-frequency transducers provide excellent resolution for imaging and close-range detection, often used in underwater exploration and detailed mapping. Each type serves distinct purposes in various aquatic applications.</p></p>
