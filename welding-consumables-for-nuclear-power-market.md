<p><p>Welding consumables for the nuclear power market include various types designed to ensure structural integrity and safety. Shielded Metal Arc Welding (SMAW) utilizes coated electrodes to create a protective gas shield, suitable for thick materials. Submerged Arc Welding (SAW) employs a granular flux to cover the arc, allowing for deep penetration and minimal spatter, ideal for large structures. Other methods may include Gas Tungsten Arc Welding (GTAW) and flux-cored arc welding, each tailored for specific applications within the nuclear industry to meet stringent quality standards.</p></p>
