<p><p>Ylang-Ylang oil is categorized based on distillation stages, each offering distinct aromatic profiles and uses. **Ylang-Ylang Extra** is the first fraction, lightest and most floral, ideal for perfumery. **Ylang-Ylang First** follows, with a slightly deeper scent, used in cosmetics. **Ylang-Ylang Second** is richer, often in soaps. **Ylang-Ylang Third** is heavier, used in aromatherapy. **Ylang-Ylang Complete** combines all fractions, offering a balanced, full-bodied aroma, popular in holistic practices. Each type caters to specific industries, reflecting its unique distillation phase and aromatic intensity.</p></p>
